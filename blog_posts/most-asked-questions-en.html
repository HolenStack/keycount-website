How well is my data in the keycount app protected from third parties (e.g. hackers)?

Your data in our app is very well protected against attacks from third parties. All communication in the keycount app is encrypted with at least 128 bits. This encryption is very secure according to current knowledge. Keycount uses banklevel security measurements to keep your data safe from third parties.

Can I close my keycount account and ensure all data is deleted?

Yes, you can delete your account in the keycount app and simultaneously request us to delete all data that we are not obliged to keep from a legal standpoint.

To delete your bank account information with our partner (aiia Master card), visit the following link: Aiia – Login

How can I connect my bank accounts?

You can enter the keycount app, click on “connect account” and then enter the relevant identifying information from your bank. The app will then forward you to the banks authentication method and subsequently your bank account and its information will be connect to the keycount app.

Which bank accounts can I connect?

You can connect bank account from over 1’520 different banks. Check the keycount app and see if your bank is available.

Which Crypto providers can I connect?

You can choose from a list of over 40 of the biggest Crypto exchanges (e.g. Binance, Coinbase, etc.) and Walletprovider (e.g. Metamask). Open the app and discover it for yourself!
