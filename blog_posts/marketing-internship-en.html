Trainee (Online-)Marketing at Fintech Startup

To support our marketing team, we are looking for a trainee with a workload of 60% to 100%, for a minimum of three months with the option of an extension.

What are your core tasks?

  • Supervision of the social media channels
  • Content creation
  • Community management
  • Coordination of competitions
  • Active co-development of marketing activities, cooperations and optimisation opportunities
  • Support with online marketing activities, communication and the creation of newsletters and press releases

Are you the person we are looking for?

  • You move confidently on LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, TikTok and co. and know your way around reels, guides and stories
  • You can inspire people with your written and spoken communication (German/English)
  • You are familiar with photo and video editing programmes and have already created your own content
  • You want to drive an innovative product forward, maybe be a part of it yourself and take on responsibility.

What do we offer you?

  • Since you will be working directly with the founders, you will have the opportunity to actively contribute and influence the success of our start-up.
  • An exciting, challenging and unusual working environment as well as a very committed team.
  • Flexible working hours and remote working.
  • Possibility of a follow-up full-time or part-time job

How do I apply?

Please send an e-mail to company@key-count.com with the subject “Application Trainee Marketing”:

  • A short introduction of yourself (3-4 sentences or a short video)
  • Your current CV
  • Your preferred start date + desired duration of the internship
  • Send us a picture post proposal for the app launch on Instagram, as you would imagine it.

We are looking forward to your application and are excited about it
